About Warplet Traders

Track the top earners using Warpcast's built-in wallet

What is Warplet Traders?

Warplet Traders tracks and displays which Warpcast users are making the most earnings with their Warplet (Warpcast's built-in wallet) on the BASE blockchain.

How does it work?

We analyze trading data from Dune Analytics and match it with social data from the Neynar API to show you who among your follows are the top earners.

What data is shown?

We show the top 5 earners, their primary token, earnings amount, and trading volume over either 24-hour or 7-day periods.

How to use the frame?

  • Click "24h Data" to see earnings from the last 24 hours
  • Click "7d Data" to see earnings from the last 7 days
  • Click "Share Results" to share with your followers

Note: This is a static version of the frame that works without requiring an API.

In the future, we'll add personalized "Check My Follows" functionality.
