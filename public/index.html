Warplet Traders

Top Warplet Traders on BASE

This app shows the top traders among the people you follow on Warpcast, based on their earnings using Warplet (Warpcast's built-in wallet).

View Frame Share on Warpcast

How It Works

The frame loads data about the top 5 earners among accounts you follow, showing:

  • Username
  • Top token traded
  • Earnings (24h and 7d views available)
  • Trading volume

You can view 24-hour or 7-day data, share results, and check who among your follows are the top traders.

Features

  • 24-hour and 7-day data views
  • Share results via Warpcast
  • "Check My Follows" to analyze your own follows
  • Real-time data from Dune Analytics
